Visit Corpus Christi

Enjoy culture, water sports, & more in this vibrant city on the Gulf of Mexico.

Top three activities to do in Corpus

Attend local attractions

The Texas State Aquarium, Art Museum or Botanical Gardens will be sure to bring you some joy!

Have fun in the sun

Rent a jetski, go kayaking (GlowRow), fish a little, or book a romantic sunset sail!

Explore our city

Gain some knowledge of our sparkling city by visiting- The USS Lexington, Surfing Museum, or Heritage Park & Cultural Center.
